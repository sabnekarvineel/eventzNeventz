# EventzNEventz - Premium Color Palette

## Overview
Updated color scheme designed for premium, rich, and trustworthy appearance. The palette balances dark and light elements for optimal visual hierarchy and readability.

## Primary Colors

| Color Name | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
|-----------|----------|-----|-------|
| **Deep Navy** | #2C3E50 | rgb(44, 62, 80) | Primary backgrounds, text, buttons, professional elements |
| **Rich Gold** | #D4AF37 | rgb(212, 175, 55) | Accent color, highlights, premium touches, hover states |
| **Sophisticated Teal** | #3B8E8C | rgb(59, 142, 140) | Section backgrounds, secondary accents |
| **Off-White/Cream** | #F5F3F0 | rgb(245, 243, 240) | Light backgrounds, body background, neutral base |

## Color Applications

### Navigation & Headers
- **Background**: Deep Navy (#2C3E50)
- **Logo/Links**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Hover Effects**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)

### Hero Section
- **Background**: Deep Navy (#2C3E50)
- **Title**: Off-White (#F5F3F0)
- **Subtitle**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Accent Orbs**: Gold & Teal with opacity

### Main Content Areas
- **About Section**: Off-White Background (#F5F3F0)
  - Headings: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)
  - Text: Deep Navy (#2C3E50)
  
- **Services/Contact/Testimonials**: Sophisticated Teal (#3B8E8C)
  - Text: White
  - Borders/Highlights: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)

### Buttons
- **Primary Button**: Deep Navy background → Rich Gold on hover
- **Secondary Button**: Gold text with gold border → Teal on hover

### Forms
- **Borders**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Text**: Deep Navy (#2C3E50)
- **Focus States**: Gold highlight with subtle shadow

### Cards & Components
- **Background**: Transparent Gold with low opacity
- **Border**: Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Hover Shadow**: Gold with transparency

### Footer
- **Background**: Deep Navy (#2C3E50)
- **Top Border**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Links Hover**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Social Icons Hover**: Gold background with Dark Navy text

## Visual Hierarchy

1. **Dominant**: Deep Navy (#2C3E50) - Trust, professionalism
2. **Accent**: Rich Gold (#D4AF37) - Premium, elegance, luxury
3. **Secondary**: Sophisticated Teal (#3B8E8C) - Modern, professional
4. **Neutral**: Off-White (#F5F3F0) - Clean, readable backgrounds

## Design Principles

✓ **Premium**: Gold accents convey luxury and high-end service
✓ **Trustworthy**: Deep Navy creates professional, reliable appearance
✓ **Balanced**: Not too dark, not too light - comfortable to view
✓ **Modern**: Teal adds contemporary sophistication
✓ **Accessible**: Strong contrast for readability
